\[ P(X=6) = \left(\begin{array}{l}6\\6\end{array}\right) 
\cdot \left(\frac{1}{49}\right)^6 \cdot \left(1 - \frac{1}{49}\right)^{6-6} 
= \frac{6!}{6! \cdot (6-6)!} \cdot \left(\frac{1}{49}\right)^6 
\cdot \left(1 - \frac{1}{49}\right)^0 = 7.22 \cdot 10^{-11} \]
