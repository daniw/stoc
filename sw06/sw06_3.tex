\subsection{a}
$\dfrac{x}{n} \pm \dfrac{1.96}{\sqrt{n}} 
\cdot \sqrt{\dfrac{x}{n} \left(1 - \dfrac{x}{n}\right)}$\\
$= \dfrac{7}{50} \pm \dfrac{1.96}{\sqrt{50}} 
\cdot \sqrt{\dfrac{7}{50} \left(1 - \dfrac{7}{50}\right)}$\\
$ = [0.0438, 0.2362]$
